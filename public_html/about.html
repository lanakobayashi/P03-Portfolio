Lana Kobayashi

Creative wanderer.

Hello! I'm Lana, a visual and user experience designer in Vancouver, Canada. Currently, I'm a third-year student studying Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University.

From jumping between one culture at home to a extremely different one in the outside environment, I've experienced the joy of producing creative works that can appeal to culturally diverse minds.

Leveraging my experience with my competencies in design, I aspire to make positive changes as a self-motivated designer and as an individual through impactful and meaningful creations.

If you are interested in hearing more about what I do, feel free to connect with me through lkobayas@sfu.ca.
